Key Insights

What Customers Value, and Where They Stall

Setup Is the Biggest Drop-off

  • Most interviewees described the first-run setup as the hardest part of getting started. 1
  • Once past setup, weekly usage climbs sharply and stays steady across the cohort. 1

Sharing Drives Repeat Use

  • Teams that shared a workspace returned roughly twice as often as solo users. 1
  • Privacy of individual chats was raised repeatedly as a precondition for sharing. 1

Trust Depends on Traceable Sources

  • Users want to jump from an answer straight to the passage it came from before acting on it. 1
  • Verification speed, not answer length, was the strongest predictor of stated confidence. 1
